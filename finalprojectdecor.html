Clean and White

In order to help achieve the clean and white aestheic look for your room, a suggested color palette to follow are earth tones.
A consistent flow of earth tones creates a simplistic and put- together space. To add pops of color, add green plants
and succulents around your room. Adding fairy lights also adds a bright and lively mood. Also, adding decorative elements the match
the color scheme creates a more personalized touch. Other options to create a more personalized atmosphere can can include adding
printed out pictures on string or on fairy lights with small clothespins. Adding decorative throw pillows and blankets also create a
cozy space. If there are different items you own that can not be incorporated to match the color scheme, you can organize them
into a miscellaneous drawer.

Rustic

A rustic bedroom has accent wood pieces. Following a similar eath toned color palette for this style bedroom
Adding spruce plant pieces around your bedroom creates a cozy and cabin- like vibe. Having many wooden
pieces can cause a room to look dark; adding fairy lights can brighten up the room. Also, a woven basket can be added
in your room for storage- such as blankets and pillows.

Indie

An essential to having an indie bedroom is having printed accessories. Mandela tapestries jazz up a room and prevent
it from being too simple. A color scheme is suggested in order to keep the room appreaing clean
and while still having several prints. Having bright colors create a lively mood; adding browns help balance out
balance the vivid colors. Adding fairy lights around your room can also help brighten it up due to the tapestries usually darkening
and closing a room. Adding printed pictures on your wall can also help personalize the room and feel closer to
the people in your life and the adventures you've gone on!.

Vintage

A vintage bedroom can incorporate antique furniture with modern touches. Adding wire framed pieces,
as well as pieces with designed frames and edges can give a vintage vibe to a room. Adding floral wallpaper
can also add to this aesthetic. Keeping a pastel color palette adds to the old timesy feel. Also, accessories
such as fake flowers and record players can make the bedroom more interesting.
